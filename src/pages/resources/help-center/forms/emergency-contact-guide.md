# Staff Guide: Emergency Contact

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/forms/emergency-contact`

## Purpose
Providing or updating emergency contact information for the patient's record.

## Required Fields
- **Patient Info**: Name, Email, Phone.
- **Emergency Contact**: Name, Relationship, Email, Phone.
- **Consent**: Must check the consent box at the bottom.

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: Confirmed immediately upon submission.
- **Staff Hint**: Remind patients that this information is only used in case of a medical emergency or safety concern.
