# Staff Guide: Guardian Information

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/forms/guardian-contact`

## Purpose
Adding legal guardian or authorized representative information for a patient.

## Required Fields
- **Patient Identification**: Standard patient info.
- **Guardian Details**: Name, Email, Phone, Relationship.
- **Legal Authorization**: Checkbox to confirm they have supporting documentation (court orders, POA, etc.).

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: Confirmed immediately upon submission.
- **Staff Hint**: Patients can add **multiple guardians** on this single form if needed.
