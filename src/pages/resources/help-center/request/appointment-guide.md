# Staff Guide: Appointment Help

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/request/appointment`

## Purpose
Rescheduling, canceling, or requesting new appointment availability.

## Required Fields
- **Subject**: Specify if rescheduling, canceling, or requesting new.
- **Message**: Details on preferred dates/times.

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: Same business day.
- **Staff Hint**: If the change is needed within less than 24 hours, encourage the patient to call the main line directly at (470) 802-6838.
