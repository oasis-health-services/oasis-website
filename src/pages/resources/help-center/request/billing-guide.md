# Staff Guide: Insurance & Billing

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/request/billing`

## Purpose
Questions about coverage, statements, copays, or payment options.

## Required Fields
- **Subject**: e.g., "Question about recent statement".
- **Message**: Details on the billing issue or insurance change.

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: 2-3 business days.
- **Staff Hint**: For direct billing support, patients can also be referred to (509) 381-6035.
