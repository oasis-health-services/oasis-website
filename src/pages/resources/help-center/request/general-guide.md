# Staff Guide: General Question

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/request/general`

## Purpose
For non-urgent questions about care, treatment options, policies, or general inquiries.

## Required Fields
- **Patient Info**: First Name, Last Name, Email, Preferred Contact.
- **Request Details**: Subject, Message.

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: 1-2 business days.
- **Note**: Most questions are ideally addressed within 24 hours.
