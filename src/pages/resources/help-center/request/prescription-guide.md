# Staff Guide: Prescription Refill

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/request/prescription`

## Purpose
Requesting medication refills or asking about dosage changes and side effects.

## Required Information
- **Medication Details**: Name, Dosage, and Frequency.
- **Pharmacy Info**: Location and contact if possible.
- **Patient Info**: Standard contact details.

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: 24-48 hours.
- **Staff Hint**: Ensure the patient knows to provide pharmacy details for faster processing.
