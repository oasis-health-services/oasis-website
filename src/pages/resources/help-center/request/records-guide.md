# Staff Guide: Medical Records

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/request/records`

## Purpose
Requesting copies of treatment records, summaries, or documentation to be sent to other providers.

## Required Fields
- **Subject**: e.g., "Records request for referral".
- **Message**: Specify which records are needed and where they should be sent.

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: 3-5 business days.
- **Staff Hint**: Mention that certain heavy records requests may take longer to process.
