# Staff Guide: Help Center Overview

This guide helps staff direct patients to the correct online form for their needs and explains what information is required.

## 📋 General Information
- **Link**: [Oasis Help Center](https://oasishealthservices.com/resources/help-center/)
- **Emergency Note**: Remind patients these forms are **for non-emergency use only**. Direct crisis situations to 911 or 988.

## Form Categories
1.  **General Question**: 1-2 business days.
2.  **Prescription Refill**: 24-48 hours.
3.  **Appointment Help**: Same business day.
4.  **Medical Records**: 3-5 business days.
5.  **Secure Document Upload**: Confirmed immediately.
6.  **Insurance & Billing**: 2-3 business days.
7.  **Emergency Contact**: Confirmed immediately.
8.  **Guardian Information**: Confirmed immediately.

*For detailed instructions on each form, refer to the specific guide in each folder.*
