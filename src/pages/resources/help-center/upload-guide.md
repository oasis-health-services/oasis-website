# Staff Guide: Secure Document Upload

**URL**: `/resources/help-center/upload`

## Purpose
Securely sending requested documents like insurance cards, ID, or prior medical records.

## Required Fields
- **Document Type**: Selecting the correct type (Insurance Card, ID, etc.).
- **Upload Area**: Drag and drop or browse for files.
- **Message**: Provide context about what is being uploaded (optional).

## Response Expectations
- **Turnaround**: Confirmed immediately upon successful upload.
- **Staff Hint**: For insurance cards, remind patients to upload **both sides** of the card.
